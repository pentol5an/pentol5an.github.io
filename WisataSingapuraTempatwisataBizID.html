Tempat Wisata Di Singapura tempatwisatabizid Khadi dvara udyana eka visala, rangina, bhavisya singapura ko khadi ksetrama parka cha. Prasid'dha Supertree sanracana bhanda akarako jahaja color akarako Greenhouses khursani pahada jalavayu puna: Sirjana ra tyaham baccaharu ra vayaska duvai lagi yo gantavya dherai rama'ilo bana'una patta laga'una rukhaharu ra biruvaharu sayaum chan, udyana bhanda eka prabhavasali skywalk prastava. Khadi dvara udyana ko akara bujhna sabai bhanda ramro sthana spesa Waterfront ma Reclaimed bhumi ko 250 ekada encompass, viparita parka sidha baschana jo merina khadi baluva ko sirsa dekhi cha. Yaham rahi tapa'im udyana ko eka ucca drsya di'era, va du'i domes najikai parka pravesa garna alikati aba nadi vicarana li'era, hotala dekhi walkways marphata ya ta, sajilo cha. Parka varipari himdne tapa'im eka pratisata kharca hunecha tara tapa'im jalavayu-niyantrana conservatories bhitra carana, va Supertrees bica hindna cahanuhuncha bhane, eka sulka cha. Mukhya nikunja ksetrama khula 9:00 Dekhi 21:00 Cha. Kunai sankalu ko bagaica ko an'ya bhagaharu ma rakhi'eko thulo dhatu kendriya nikunja ma sanracana ra adha darjana aruko 12 khadi dvara gardana towering, ispata-phrema'rukha' kheta ko prabhutva cha.
